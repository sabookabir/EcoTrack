# 🌎 EcoTrack AI

### Track. Reduce. Regrow.

EcoTrack AI is a next-generation Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform designed to help individuals, communities, colleges, and organizations understand, track, and reduce their environmental impact through intelligent analytics, personalized recommendations, gamification, and sustainability-focused insights.

Built with a modern climate-tech approach, EcoTrack AI transforms carbon tracking from a boring calculation into an engaging sustainability journey.

---

## 🌱 Problem Statement

Climate change is one of the biggest global challenges of our time.

Most people contribute to carbon emissions through transportation, energy consumption, food choices, and purchasing habits, but very few understand the actual environmental impact of their daily decisions.

Existing carbon calculators often provide a single number without helping users understand what it means or how they can improve.

EcoTrack AI bridges this gap by providing:

* Carbon footprint tracking
* Environmental impact visualization
* AI-powered sustainability recommendations
* Community engagement
* Gamified eco challenges
* Long-term progress monitoring
